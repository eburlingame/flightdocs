# Commercial Pilot Privileges and Limitations

The holder of a commercial pilot certificate may act as pilot in command of an
aircraft for **compensation or hire**, provided the person is qualified in accordance with
this part and with the applicable parts of this chapter that apply to the operation.

- A commercial pilot is free to work _for_ a [commercial operator](/topics/commercial/operators), who is certificated for
  the operations they perform.
- They may also act _as_ an operator themselves, but the services their offer may require a [operating certificate](/topics/commercial/operators).

Limitations:

- Without an instrument rating, a commercial pilot may not carry passengers:
  - At night
  - On cross-country flights in excess of **50nm**
