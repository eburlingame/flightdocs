# Convective Outlooks

Convective outlooks are issued by the storm prediction center, and include the
_likelihood_ of severe weather over the next **8** days. This weather includes:

- Severe convective activity
  - Tornados
  - Wind gusts 50 kts or greater
  - Hail 1 in in diameter or greater)
- Non-severe/general convective activity

<img {...convectiveOutlook}></img>

Note that the marginal or slight areas indicates a marginal or slight
_potential_ for severe thunderstorms, not "marginal" thunderstorms.
