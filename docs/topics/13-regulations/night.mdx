# Night

Depending on the context, the FAA has several definitions of "night".

## For logging night time

Night time may be logged between the end of **evening civil twilight** and the
start of **morning civil twilight**.

<Reg showTitle part="1" />

## For logging takeoffs and landings at night

To meet recent experience requirements, takeoffs and landings at night must be
made between:

- **1 hour after sunset**, to
- **1 hour before sunrise**

<Reg part="61.57(b)" />

## For Position Lights

An aircraft's position lights shall be operated between **sunset** and
**sunrise**.

<Reg part="91.209" />
