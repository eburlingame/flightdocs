# Overbanking Tendency

In a turn your outer wing moves slightly faster through the air then the inner
wing. This creates asymmetric lift, causing the aircraft to exhibit an
over-banking tendency. You may need opposite aileron to maintain your bank
angle, and prevent over-banking.
