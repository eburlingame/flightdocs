# Motion Sickness

Motion sickness, or airsickness, is caused by the brain receiving conflicting
messages about the state of the body.

## Symptoms

- General discomfort
- Nausea
- Dizziness
- Paleness
- Sweating
- Vomiting

## Treatment

- Opening fresh air vents
- Focusing on objects outside the airplane
- Avoiding unnecessary head movements
