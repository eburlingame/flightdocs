# Record-keeping

Each person shall document the following time in a manner "acceptable to the
Administrator":

- Training and experience used for the requirements of a **certificate, rating,
  or flight review**
- The experience required for meeting the **recent flight experience**
  requirements

Logbook entries shall include:

- General information
  - Date
  - Total flight time or lesson time
  - Location where the aircraft departed and arrived
    - For lessons in a flight simulator, the location where the lesson occurred
  - Type and identification of aircraft or flight simulator
  - The name of a safety pilot (if required)
- Type of experience or training
  - Solo
  - Pilot in command
  - Second in command
  - Flight and ground training received from an authorized instructor
  - Training received in a full flight simulator, flight training device, or
    aviation training device from an authorized instructor
- Flight conditions
  - Day or night.
  - Actual instrument.
  - Simulated instrument conditions in flight, or flight simulator

<Footnotes>
  \* Night is defined as the period during the period beginning 1 hour after
  sunset and ending 1 hour before sunrise
</Footnotes>
