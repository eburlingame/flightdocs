## No Portable Electronic Devices

No person may operate a portable electronic device:

- In an aircraft operated by a holder of an operating certificate
- Any aircraft operting under IFR

Except for the following device:

- Portable voice recorders
- Hearing aids
- Heart pace makers
- Electric shavers
- Any other device that the operator has determined will not interfere with the
  navigation or communication systems on the aircraft
