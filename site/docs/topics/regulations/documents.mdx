# Physical Possession of Pilot Documents

To act as a "pilot flight crewmember" of a civil aircraft unless they in their
physical possession:

- A pilot certificate or temporary certificate
- Photo identification, including a:
  - State driver's license
  - Passport
  - Military identification card
  - Government identification card
- A valid medical certificate

# Lost or Destroyed Documents

If a pilot certificate is lost or destroyed, the FAA should be notified by mail,
and they can issue a 60-day temporary certificate.

# Address Changes

A pilot should notify the FAA within 30 days of a change of address.
