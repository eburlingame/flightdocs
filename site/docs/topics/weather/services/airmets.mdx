# AIRMETs

AIRMETs are weather advisories issued for significant weather that does not meet
the criteria for a [SIGMET](/topics/weather/services/airmets).

They are issued every 6 hours.

## AIRMET Sierra: IFR or Mountain Obscuration

AIRMET Sierras are issued for:

- Ceilings < 1000' and/or < 3 mi visibility affecting more than 50% of the area
- Extensive mountain obscuration

## AIRMET Tango: Turbulence

AIRMET Tangos are issued for:

- Moderate high altitude turbulence (> FL180)
- Moderate low level turbulence (< FL180)
- Low level wind shear (LLWS) below 2000 feet AGL
- Sustained surface winds of 30 knots or more at the surface

## AIRMET Zulu: Icing

AIRMET Zulus are issued for:

- Moderate icing
- Lowest freezing levels
- Multiple freezing levels
