# Graphical Area Forecasts

Graphical Area Forecasts are replacement for the text-base Area Forecasts, and
display forecasts and hazards for the continental US in a single chart. The
online tool shows flight category, surface wind along with SIGMETs and AIRMETs
for up to **15** hours in the future.
