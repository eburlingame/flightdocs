# Center Weather Advisories (CWAs)

A Center Weather Advisory (CWA) is an unscheduled weather advisory for
conditions meeting or approaching national in-flight advisory (WA, WS, or WST)
criteria. It is primarily used by aircrews to anticipate and avoid adverse
weather conditions in the en route and terminal environments.
